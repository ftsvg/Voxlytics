Search Player

Players

Voxlytics provides beautiful stat profiles for all players on the Voxyl Network. Use /stats [player] [mode] to view detailed in-game statistics. Link your account with /link [player] to make running commands faster and easier.

Leaderboards

Voxlytics lets you explore competitive leaderboards across the Voxyl Network. Use /leaderboard level [page] or /leaderboard weightedwins [page] to browse rankings.

Historical

Voxlytics includes historical tracking, allowing you to view player progress over time. Use /historical daily [player], /historical weekly [player], /historical monthly [player], or /historical yearly [player] to track stats across different time periods. You can reset your historical data with /historical reset.

Sessions

Voxlytics sessions let you track your progress in real time across play sessions. Each user can have only one active session, and you must be linked to create or view session data. Use /session view to see your current session stats, or /session reset to clear your session and start fresh.

Projected

Voxlytics Prestige allows you to project a player's future stats based on their session stats. Use /prestige [level] [player] to calculate estimated progression and stat outcomes from an active session. Please note that a session must be started first using /session start [session] before prestige data can be generated.

Compare

Voxlytics lets you compare player stats side by side to see who comes out on top. Use /compare [player_1] [player_2] to instantly view a detailed comparison of their stats.
